What is a Signal?

6 weeks
immersion

"In media, a signal is what cuts through the noise — it is clear, intentional, and worth paying attention to. Every fellow in this program is learning to be that for the brands and communities they serve."

Work on real briefs, and launch a real career in digital marketing.

Program Structure

Three phases.
Six weeks. Real work.

The program is not theory-heavy. Every session produces a real output. Every phase builds on the last.

PHASE 1

The Intensive

WEEKS 1 – 3

  • Week 1: Copywriting & headlines that hook
  • Week 2: Platform mechanics & audience growth
  • Week 3: Brand storytelling & camera confidence
  • Daily headline drills and live critique sessions
  • Guest session from Kampala industry practitioner

PHASE 2

Live Briefs

WEEKS 4 – 5

  • Real briefs from actual brand partners
  • Weekly submission and peer critique
  • Coach feedback on every deliverable
  • Community management deep-dive
  • Analytics and reporting fundamentals

PHASE 3

Portfolio & Showcase

WEEK 6

  • Finalise 3 polished portfolio pieces
  • Case study write-up & LinkedIn update
  • Rehearsal and presentation coaching
  • Public showcase at era92 Signal Showcase event
  • Industry judges and live feedback

Who It's For

Skill-Driven Creators

Committed Kampala Participants
